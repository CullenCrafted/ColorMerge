How to Play

1. There are 5 color buttons below; Blue, Red, Yellow, White, & Black

2. Try to match the background by mixing these colors

3. Each color button press will add that color to the mix (center circle)

4. The small gray dots and the quantities of them (the Level), represent the number of colors that mixed to make the background

5. You'll start with 1 color, if correct, then 2 colors, and so on

5. In some scenarios it's possible to match the background in fewer tries; you'll recieve an additional heart

6. Lose a heart for every incorrect match, and gain a heart every 10 levels

7. Have fun!
